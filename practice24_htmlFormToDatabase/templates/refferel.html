    Care & Connects is an Australian disability support agency providing ‘Person centered’ care. Our experienced, dynamic, and professional team is here to help you to act self-sufficiently, empowering you to access your supports. We provide support to help you to engage more in your community and live Your life, your way. At Care & Connects we attracts, rewards, and retains highly skilled staff to provide excellent services to our clients. Our proactive approach is to develop a unique and extensive person-centered approach to every individual.
